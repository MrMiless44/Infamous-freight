## Common Questions Answered

### Q: How fast do I get paid?

**A**: 1-2 business days for standard daily deposits. Instant payout available for 1% fee.

### Q: Do I pay Stripe directly?

**A**: No, Stripe deducts their fee (2.9%) from what they deposit to your account. It's automatic.

### Q: What if there's a chargeback?

**A**: Stripe handles it. They deduct the chargeback amount from a future deposit. Happens rarely (<0.5%).

### Q: Can I withdraw early?

**A**: Yes, anytime. Manual withdraw via Stripe dashboard. Instant payout available.

### Q: What if I need emergency money?

**A**: Instant payout: 1-2 hours, 1% fee. Or manual withdrawal: 1-2 days, no fee.

### Q: How much should I keep as buffer?

**A**: 3-6 months operating costs. At $131K/month costs = $393K-$786K buffer.

### Q: Do I need an accountant?

**A**: Strongly recommended. They'll save you $30K-$100K+ in taxes annually.

### Q: What about quarterly taxes?

**A**: Pay 25% of profit quarterly (Jan 15, Apr 15, Jun 15, Sep 15). Your CPA handles calculations.

### Q: Can I move money to personal account?

**A**: Yes, anytime. Transfer from business checking to personal checking.

### Q: Is there a minimum withdrawal?

**A**: No. Even $1 can be withdrawn. But standard deposits are daily auto-batches.
