## 💡 Critical Concepts

### 1. Daily Automatic Deposits
Your Stripe account is connected to your business bank account. Every business day (Mon-Fri), all revenue from that day is automatically deposited. **No minimum amount, no action required.**

You can see:
- Every transaction in Stripe (instantly)
- Every deposit in your bank (within 1-2 days)
- Complete cost breakdown (operations, taxes)
