# Refunds & Chargebacks Policy

Refunds are issued only when:
- Fraud is confirmed by the processor OR
- A verified platform billing error exists.

Refunds are not issued for:
- Delivered services
- AI usage
- Digital goods
- Subscription periods already accessed.

Unauthorized chargebacks are considered a breach of payment terms.

# Account Enforcement Policy

Infæmous Freight reserves the right to:
- Restrict
- Suspend
- Terminate

Any account found in violation of:
- Payment integrity
- KYC requirements
- Platform security
- Fraud prevention standards
