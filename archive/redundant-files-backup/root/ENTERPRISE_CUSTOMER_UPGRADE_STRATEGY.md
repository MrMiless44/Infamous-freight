## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk: Customers say "too expensive" ($99/month jump from Free)

**Mitigation**:

- Emphasize savings (2.5 hrs/load × $50/hr × 100 loads = $12.5K/month saved)
- Offer annual discount (20% off = $948/year = save $240)
- 14-day risk-free trial (reduce perceived risk)
- Seasonal pricing (offer 50% off for Q1 2026 only)

### Risk: Churn spike if pricing seen as aggressive

**Mitigation**:

- Grandfather existing customers (no price increase)
- Announce: "Free tier will remain forever"
- New customers pay new prices; existing customers locked in
- Emphasize value-add (not price increase)

### Risk: Low conversion rate (<20%)

**Mitigation**:

- A/B test email subject lines (measure 50% improvement)
- Personalize offer to each customer's usage pattern
- Sales team direct outreach (not just email)
- Escalation: CEO calls top 10 highest-revenue customers
