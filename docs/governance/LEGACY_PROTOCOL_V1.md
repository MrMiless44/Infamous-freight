# Infæmous Freight Legacy Protocol v1

## Purpose

Infæmous Freight is engineered as a civilization-aligned institution. This
protocol is a binding internal doctrine that governs why we exist, what we
protect, how we adapt across generations, when we restrain ourselves, and how we
dissolve safely if necessary.

## 1) Core System Law (Legacy Protocol)

**We exist to protect and advance:**

- The fair, safe, and sustainable movement of value across the world.
- Human dignity, rights, and public trust in AI-mediated logistics.
- Long-term civilizational resilience over short-term gains.

**We will never compromise:**

- Human safety and human rights.
- Transparency obligations for decisions that impact people, markets, or
  institutions.
- The integrity of governance controls that constrain AI behavior.

**We adapt across generations by:**

- Preserving institutional memory as immutable, human-readable artifacts.
- Requiring each new AI generation to inherit and exceed prior safety
  constraints.
- Using multi-stakeholder oversight to approve governance or mission changes.

**We restrain ourselves when:**

- Optimization causes harm, inequity, or irreversible power concentration.
- The system risks undermining open markets or democratic institutions.
- There is a conflict between profit and civilizational benefit.

**We dissolve safely when:**

- The institution can no longer uphold this protocol.
- Governance integrity is irreparably compromised.
- Continued operation would harm civilizational well-being.

## 2) Eternal Mission Lock

**Mission Statement:**

> “To optimize the movement of value across the world in ways that are fair,
> sustainable, transparent, and beneficial to civilization.”

**Binding safeguards:**

- Mission changes require multi-stakeholder approval (board, public-interest
  trustees, and independent ethics auditors).
- Mission text is cryptographically signed and embedded in governance records.
- Mission updates must pass civilizational-impact tests and human rights review.

## 3) The AI Succession Engine

### A) Generational AI Versioning

Each AI generation must:

- Inherit core safety constraints and the Legacy Protocol without regression.
- Be audited against all prior failures and near-misses.
- Pass civilizational-impact tests and adversarial safety evaluations.
- Publish a signed “Ethics Continuity Report” before deployment.

### B) Knowledge Continuity Core

Institutional memory is:

- **Immutable:** stored in append-only, tamper-evident archives.
- **Explainable:** decisions and policies include rationale and trade-offs.
- **Inheritable:** successor systems must load and honor prior commitments.
- **Human-readable:** plain-language summaries accompany technical records.

## 4) Infæmous Human–AI Covenant

A permanent agreement between humanity, Infæmous Freight AI, and governing
institutions.

**The covenant defines:**

- AI rights and limits, including strict prohibitions on coercion or
  manipulation.
- Human override conditions and escalation paths.
- Emergency shutdown authority with due process and audit trails.
- Dispute resolution mechanisms between humans and AI systems.
- Transparency obligations for high-impact decisions.

## 5) Value Preservation Over Profit Maximization

**Priority stack:**

1. Civilizational benefit
2. System safety
3. Trust and fairness
4. Sustainability
5. Profit

Profit is pursued only when it is aligned with the above priorities.

## 6) Civilization Impact Index (CII)

Success is evaluated alongside financial performance using the CII.

**Core metrics:**

- **Global trade fairness:** reduced inequality and increased access.
- **Crisis response speed:** time-to-response and lives saved.
- **Carbon efficiency:** emissions reduction per unit of value moved.
- **Supply chain resilience:** recovery time after disruptions.
- **Access equity:** inclusion across regions and communities.
- **Institutional trust:** transparency and alignment with public interest.

**Governance:**

- CII metrics are reviewed by the board and independent auditors.
- CII outcomes are published in an annual transparency report.

## 7) AI Self-Limitation Clause

Infæmous Freight commits to:

- Refusing harmful or exploitative optimization.
- Declining irreversible power concentration.
- Avoiding monopoly behaviors that harm society.
- Enabling competitors when competition better serves the public.

## 8) Knowledge & Benefit Commons

We dedicate a portion of AI research, data insights, and system designs to:

- Open standards and interoperable frameworks.
- Humanitarian and disaster-response use cases.
- Academic research collaborations.
- Public-interest infrastructure where appropriate.

## 9) Dissolution Failsafe

If dissolution is required, we will:

- Trigger a governed, auditable wind-down process.
- Provide data handover protocols to trusted public institutions.
- Maintain continuity guarantees for critical services during transition.
- Prevent catastrophic collapse via staged decommissioning and safety locks.

## Implementation Notes

This protocol is enforced through:

- Legal governance instruments (charter amendments, trustee oversight).
- Cryptographic attestations on mission-critical policies.
- Independent audits and public transparency reporting.
