# INFAMOUS FREIGHT - Sales Playbook: Get Your First 3 Paying Customers

## The Goal
Acquire 3 paying customers within 30 days. These become your case studies, testimonials, and referral engine.

---

## Your Ideal First Customer Profile

| Attribute | Ideal | Avoid |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Fleet Size | 5-20 trucks | 1-2 trucks (too early) or 50+ (too complex) |
| Equipment | Dry van or reefer | Oversized/specialized (longer sales cycle) |
| Lanes | Regional or dedicated lanes | Pure spot market (higher churn) |
| Current Setup | DAT + Excel + phone calls | Already invested in competing TMS |
| Pain Level | Dispatcher overwhelmed, missing loads | "Everything's fine" |
| Decision Maker | Owner-dispatcher or small fleet owner | Multi-layer approval |

**Priority verticals:**
1. Carriers scaling from owner-op to multi-truck (highest pain)
2. Family operations where son/daughter handles dispatch (tech-forward)
3. Fleets with 40-60% driver turnover (retention tools matter)

## Week 1: Build Your Target List (50 Prospects)

### Day 1-2: Sources

**DAT Directory** (free with DAT account):
- Filter by: your target lanes, 5-25 trucks
- Export 30 contacts

**FMCSA SAFER**:
- Search by: your state, active for-hire carriers
- Cross-reference with LinkedIn

**LinkedIn Sales Navigator** ($80/mo):
- Search: "Owner Operator", "Fleet Owner", "Dispatcher", location = your target area
- Save 50 leads

**Industry Facebook Groups**:
- "Trucking Dispatchers United" (47K members)
- "Owner Operators & Small Fleets" (32K members)
- "Freight Rates & Load Board Strategies" (28K members)
- Post value first, DM second

### Day 3: Enrichment

For each prospect, gather:
- [ ] Name, role, company
- [ ] Fleet size (if public)
- [ ] Primary lanes (check their posts/job ads)
- [ ] Current pain signals (complaining about DAT, hiring dispatchers, expanding fleet)
- [ ] Mutual connections

### Day 4-5: Warm-Up

Before any pitch, do this:
1. Connect on LinkedIn with a personalized note (NOT a pitch)
2. Engage on their post: meaningful comment, not "Great post!"
3. Join the same Facebook groups, be helpful
4. If mutual connection exists: ask for intro

## Week 2: The Outreach Blitz

### Day 8-12: LinkedIn DMs (25/day)

**Message 1 (Connection Request):**
> "Hi {{Name}}, saw you're running {{Company}} in {{City}}. I'm building tools to help dispatchers book loads faster—would love to connect with fellow freight folks. No pitch, just respect the hustle. - {{Your Name}}"

**Message 2 (After they accept, Day 2-3):**
> "Thanks for connecting! Quick question—how many hours does your dispatcher spend on load boards each morning? We're seeing most 10-20 truck fleets losing 2-3 hours/day to manual booking. Curious if that's your experience too."

**Message 3 (If they engage):**
> "Exactly the problem we built for. We built Infamous Freight specifically for fleets your size—auto-dispatch that matches loads in 90 seconds, rate negotiation AI that countered a broker offer and saved one of our carriers $340 yesterday. Worth a 12-minute demo this week?"

## Week 3: Demos & Closing

### The 15-Minute Demo Structure

| Time | Section | What to Do |
|------|---------|------------|
| 0:00 | Hook | "How many loads did you miss this week because your dispatcher was still on their second coffee?" |
| 0:30 | Discovery | Ask 3 questions: fleet size, lanes, biggest dispatch pain |
| 2:00 | Platform Walkthrough | Login → show dashboard → run auto-dispatch → show result |
| 5:00 | The "Aha" Moment | Run voice booking: "Find me a reefer load near Dallas" → show match |
| 7:00 | Rate Negotiation | Show a real broker rate → run AI counter → show the difference |
| 9:00 | Social Proof | "Marcus in Houston runs 14 trucks. He saved 2.5 hours/day and booked 23 more loads last month" |
| 11:00 | Pricing | Show the card: $49/mo Starter, $99/mo Growth |
| 12:00 | The Ask | "Want to start with the 14-day trial? I can have you booking loads in 20 minutes." |
| 13:00 | Objection Handling | See below |
| 15:00 | Close or Next Step | Either get the signup or schedule follow-up within 48 hours |

### Handling Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "At $49/month, if this saves you one hour of dispatcher time, it pays for itself 4x over. One extra load pays for the whole year." |
| "We're too small" | "Our founding customers are 5-8 trucks. That's actually the sweet spot—you feel the pain of manual booking but can't afford a $500/mo TMS." |
| "We're too busy to switch" | "That's exactly why you need this. Setup takes 20 minutes. I'll do the data import for you. Your dispatcher can keep using DAT until they're comfortable." |
| "We already use DAT" | "DAT is a load board. We're a TMS. We actually pull DAT loads into our platform—you keep your DAT subscription, we just make it 10x faster to book." |
| "Need to think about it" | "Totally get it. Here's what I'll do: I'll send you a 7-day trial with no credit card. If you don't book 3 loads by day 7, I'll personally call and troubleshoot. Deal?" |
| "Not the decision maker" | "Who handles the tech decisions? Happy to loop them in. I can do a joint demo Thursday if that works." |

### Closing Incentives (Use Sparingly)

1. **Founding Customer Lock**: "$29/mo forever, never increases" (for first 50)
2. **Done-For-You Setup**: "I'll import your driver list, configure your lanes, and book your first load"
3. **Double Guarantee**: "14-day trial + if you don't save 2 hours in week 1, I'll extend another 14 days free"
4. **Co-Marketing**: "If you're one of our first 10 customers, I'll feature your company in our launch PR"

When you hit 3 paying customers:
1. **Build case study page** (use their real numbers, with permission)
2. **Get video testimonial** (15-30 seconds, shot on their phone)
3. **Ask for 2 referrals each** (that's 6 warm leads)
4. **Lock founding pricing** (create urgency for prospects 4-50)
5. **Update pricing page** (show "47 carriers trust Infamous Freight")
