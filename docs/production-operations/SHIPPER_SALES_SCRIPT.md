# Shipper Sales Script

## Cold Call Opener

Hi, this is [Name] with Infamous Freight.

We help businesses move freight through reliable carrier sourcing, dispatch coordination, shipment tracking, and delivery documentation.

Do you currently move full truckload, LTL, expedited, regional, or long-haul freight?

## Discovery Questions

- What lanes are you moving most often?
- What equipment do you usually need?
- Where do you run into the most problems?
- Is reliability, communication, price, or tracking the bigger issue?
- Who handles freight decisions on your team?

## Close

Send me one lane you move regularly, and I will put together a competitive quote so you can compare us against your current option.
