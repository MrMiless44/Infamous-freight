# Insurance Overview

The Infæmous Freight Insurance Center provides a centralized workflow for
certificate intake, compliance evaluation, and embedded insurance monetization.
It supports core coverage lines (CGL, Cyber, E&O, Contingent Cargo) with
extensible coverage types as the platform grows.

## Primary Workflows

- Certificate upload and verification
- Coverage requirements configuration per organization
- Compliance evaluation with warning, non-compliant, and suspension states
- Embedded per-load and business policy quote requests

## Key Outcomes

- Reduced onboarding friction for carriers
- Automated compliance monitoring
- Investor-ready audit logging of insurance decisions
