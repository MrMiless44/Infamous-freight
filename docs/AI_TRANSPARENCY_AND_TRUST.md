# AI Transparency & Trust

This guidance defines the UI copy and behavioral requirements for every AI
suggestion in Infæmous Freight Enterprises.

## Explainability Copy (Required)

Display a "Why this suggestion?" panel with bullet reasons:

- Based on lane history
- Driver availability
- Current weather risk
- Time-to-delivery optimization

## Confidence Indicator

Show a clear confidence label on every AI suggestion:

- Low
- Medium
- High

## Human Override

Provide a clear override action:

- **Override & Assign Manually**

Overrides are logged for auditability and continuous improvement. No penalty or
throttling is applied for overrides.
