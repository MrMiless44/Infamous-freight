# Product Hunt Launch Day Checklist

## Pre-Launch (7 Days Before)

- [ ] Confirm hunter (ideally someone with 500+ followers in SaaS/freight)
- [ ] Prepare gallery: 5 screenshots + 1 GIF (auto-dispatch in action)
- [ ] Write first comment (founder story + demo video link)
- [ ] Prepare maker response templates for common questions
- [ ] Notify "Founding 50" list: launch date + special PH pricing
- [ ] Schedule Product Hunt "Coming Soon" page
- [ ] Create day-of Slack/Discord channel for team coordination

## Launch Day (D-Day)

### 12:01 AM PST (Launch Moment)
- [ ] Verify page is live, all links work
- [ ] Post first comment (founder story)
- [ ] Share on personal LinkedIn + Twitter/X
- [ ] Send email to founding 50 list with PH link

### 6:00 AM PST (Morning Push)
- [ ] Post in 5 relevant Facebook trucking groups (soft, not spammy)
- [ ] Share on Hacker News "Show HN"
- [ ] Email newsletter subscribers
- [ ] LinkedIn company post with PH link

### 9:00 AM PST (Mid-Morning)
- [ ] Reply to EVERY comment within 10 minutes
- [ ] Ask 3 friendly makers to hunt/upvote/comment
- [ ] Post demo video as reply to top comment
- [ ] Share on Reddit r/freight, r/trucking

### 12:00 PM PST (Afternoon)
- [ ] Check ranking, adjust messaging if needed
- [ ] Post "Rate this load" feature as separate comment highlight
- [ ] Twitter thread: "5 things I learned building a TMS"
- [ ] Engage with other products launched same day (reciprocity)

### 3:00 PM PST (Late Push)
- [ ] Final LinkedIn post: "We're #X on Product Hunt today"
- [ ] Email any carriers who signed up today personally
- [ ] Thank every commenter individually

### 6:00 PM PST (End of Day)
- [ ] Capture final ranking
- [ ] Download all new signups
- [ ] Send personal thank-you to top upvoters/commenters
- [ ] Plan "Day 2" follow-up content

## Post-Launch (Day 2-7)

- [ ] Day 2: "Behind the scenes" blog post
- [ ] Day 3: Case study from first trial signup
- [ ] Day 4: Feature highlight (voice booking)
- [ ] Day 5: Rate comparison tool demo
- [ ] Day 6: Founder AMA on IndieHackers
- [ ] Day 7: Weekly metrics report + "week 1" reflection

## Success Metrics

| Goal | Target |
|------|--------|
| Product Hunt ranking | Top 5 |
| Upvotes | 200+ |
| Comments | 50+ |
| Trial signups from PH | 20+ |
| Paying customers from PH | 3+ |
