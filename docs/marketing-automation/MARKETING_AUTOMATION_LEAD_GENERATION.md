  • 80-100 points: Hot lead (hand off to sales immediately)

  • 40-59 points: Cold lead (email nurture campaign)
  • 0-39 points: Unqualified (exclude from paid outreach)

ACTION TRIGGERS:
  • Hot lead: Sales notification (Slack alert, email)
  • Warm lead: Book demo CTA (automated email)
  • Cold lead: Add to nurture campaign
