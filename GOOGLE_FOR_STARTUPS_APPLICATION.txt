WHY DID YOU START THIS COMPANY?
---
I witnessed firsthand how independent truck drivers—the backbone of our economy—are systematically disadvantaged by outdated technology and broker-controlled marketplaces. 4.2 million owner-operators lose 30-40% of their potential earnings because they lack access to the same intelligence and automation that billion-dollar fleets use.

The freight industry is the last major sector untouched by modern AI and product-led growth. I saw an opportunity to build the "Shopify meets ChatGPT" for logistics—democratizing enterprise-grade technology through a freemium model and AI-powered intelligence.

After seeing the logistics market up close, I realized no incumbent could make this transition. Legacy TMS companies can't do freemium (it cannibalizes their existing business), and marketplaces can't do SaaS (they make money on transaction cuts, not subscriptions). This required a greenfield AI-native platform built by someone who understood both the technology and the market dynamics.

The vision: give every owner-operator the same AI-powered intelligence that Fortune 500 logistics companies have, for free or $99/month. Level the playing field. That's why I started INFÆMOUS FREIGHT.
