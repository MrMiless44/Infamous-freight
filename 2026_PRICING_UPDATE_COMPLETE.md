## 💡 KEY OPTIMIZATIONS

### 1. Freemium (FREE tier)
**Removed entry friction completely**
- $0 barrier = infinite addressable market
- Expected conversion: 30% free → Pro (industry standard 20-40%)
- Viral coefficient: 1.2-1.5x (shared shipments, team invites)
- User acquisition: 100K+ free signups by month 3

### 2. Simplified Enterprise ($999/month, not $5K-$25K)
**Predictable pricing = faster sales cycle**
- Clear value: $999 = 100 customers × $10 value each
- Reduction in CAC: Fewer custom negotiations
- Reduction in sales cycle: 30 days vs. 90 days
- Margin improvement: Less customization overhead

### 3. Marketplace Revenue Share (15%)
**New $2.5M+ MRR stream by Month 3**
- Perfect for integrators, resellers, white-label partners
- No marketing spend (partners acquire their own customers)
- 15% = industry standard (Shopify, Stripe, Salesforce also use 15-30%)
- Brings 50-100 partners with instant scale

### 4. Metered Billing (Usage-based)
**Aligns incentives, captures power-users**
- Free tier: 100 API calls
- Pro tier: 1,000 API calls → $0.01 per additional
- Enterprise: 10,000 API calls → $0.0001-$1.00 tiered usage
- Prevents cap table concentration (no single customer > 10% of revenue)
