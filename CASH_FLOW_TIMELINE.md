## WITHDRAWAL OPTIONS & TIMELINES

### Option 1: Automatic Daily Deposits (Default) ⭐
```
Setup:
1. Log into Stripe
2. Go to Settings → Payouts
3. Select "Daily" schedule
4. Connect your bank account
5. Verify with deposits

How it works:
├── Every business day (Mon-Fri)
├── Stripe deposits that day's revenue
├── Available in your bank next morning
├── Automatic - no action needed
└── $0 fees

Example:
Tuesday 3 PM    → $21,924 in transactions
Wednesday 9 AM  → $21,924 in your bank account
Wednesday onwards → You can withdraw anytime

Advantages:
✅ No minimum withdrawal amount
✅ Funds available almost daily
✅ Predictable cash flow
✅ Maximum liquidity
✅ Zero fees
```

### Option 2: Weekly Deposits
```
Setup:
1. Stripe Settings → Payouts
2. Select "Weekly" (e.g., Monday)
3. Confirm

How it works:
├── Every Monday
├── Deposits all revenue from prior week
├── 1 business day to arrive
└── $0 fees

Example:
Week of Nov 4-10 → $145,271 collected
Monday Nov 11, 9 AM → Stripe initiates transfer
Tuesday Nov 11, 10 AM → Your bank receives funds

Best for: Larger sums, less frequent transfers
```

### Option 3: Manual Withdrawal (Instant Access)
```
Setup:
Any time you need money, log into Stripe

How it works:
1. Go to Stripe Dashboard
2. Navigate to "Payouts"
3. Click "Create instant payout"
4. Select amount (up to your available balance)
5. Confirm bank account
6. Receive funds in 1-2 hours

Cost: 1% fee ($1 per $100 withdrawn)

Example:
Available balance: $185,000
Withdraw: $10,000 for emergency
Fee: $100 (1%)
Arrives in: 1-2 hours

Use case: 
- Emergency cash needs
- Time-sensitive expenses
- Large lump sums
```

### Option 4: International Transfers (ACH/Wire)
```
Setup:
Bank transfer settings in Stripe

How it works:
1. Request ACH or wire transfer
2. Stripe processes next business day
3. International wire: 3-5 days
4. Cost: $0-$25 depending on bank

Ideal for:
├── Non-US accounts
├── Large bulk transfers
├── Higher security needs
└── Schedule transfers in advance
```
