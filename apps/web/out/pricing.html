FAQ

What is an AI action?

Do you warn before overages?
Yes—alerts trigger at 80% usage in the dashboard + email/SMS if enabled.
What happens at the hard cap?
AI automation pauses at 200% of included usage unless you upgrade or approve higher limits.
How does Enterprise billing work?
Invoice-first with contract terms. ACH preferred. You can still attach usage billing.
