## RESPONSE SCENARIOS & HOW TO HANDLE

### Scenario 1: "Love it, but we typically invest at Series B"
**Response**:
"Great feedback. We actually expect to raise Series B in 6 months when we hit $50M+ ARR. Would love to stay in touch. Meanwhile, can you intro me to [fund that does Series A]?"

### Scenario 2: "Margins seem too high, how's churn?"
**Response**:
"Great question. Cohort N (first 100 Free→Pro users) has 95% retention at Month 2. We're tracking retention obsessively. Happy to share cohort analysis — want to see it?"

### Scenario 3: "Who else is investing?"
**Response**:
"We intentionally haven't disclosed lead investor yet. But we have strong interest from [category of funds]. Want to be part of the syndicate?"

### Scenario 4: "Can you get us to $2M raise instead of $1.5M?"
**Response**:
"Open to it. Main question: what would the extra $500K accelerate? We're already hiring sales + launching marketplace. But happy to extend to [specific use case]."

### Scenario 5: "Pass"
**Response**:
"Appreciate the honesty. Would love feedback: what would make you a yes? Happy to stay in touch as we hit milestones."
