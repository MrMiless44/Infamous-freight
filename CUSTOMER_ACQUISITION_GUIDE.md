## 📞 OUTBOUND SALES PROCESS

### Phase 1: Prospecting (Week 1-2)

**Build Target List:**

For owner-operators:
```

1. Reddit r/Truckers (find active members)
```

For small fleets:
```
Sources:
1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator
   Filter: 
   - Title: "Dispatcher", "Operations", "Fleet Manager"
   - Company size: 2-50
   - Industry: Transportation, Logistics

3. Google Maps search "fleet dispatch [city]"
4. TrukerPath/Convoy (fleet profiles)
5. DAT Freight board (shipper backhauls)

Build list of 100 companies:
- Column A: Company name
- Column B: Decision-maker name
- Column C: Email + phone
- Column D: Company size
- Column E: Pain points (from research)

Warm outreach (find mutual connections on LinkedIn first)
```

**Warm-up Sequence (Pre-pitch):**
```
Week 1:
- Day 1: Follow on social (Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit)
- Day 2: Like/comment on their posts (genuine engagement)
- Day 3: Direct message (brief, no pitch)
  "Hey [Name], love what you shared about [specific topic]. 
   That's exactly the problem we're solving. 
   Curious what your biggest blocker is?"

Day 4-5: Wait for response
If no response: Don't pitch yet
If response: Continue conversation (not sales)
```
